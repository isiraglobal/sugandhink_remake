The Scent Profile

A singular olfactive formulation. Maturing for over twelve months in our dark cellars in Malad, this perfume explores complex raw materials sourced with high devotion.

Historical Inspiration

Hand-extracted via the ancient Deg-Bhapka steam distillation method in Kannauj, Uttar Pradesh. This bottle represents an unyielding devotion to historical olfaction, retaining the rich, unpolished soul of natural botanicals.

Top Notes
Bergamot · Cardamom

The immediate, volatile opening that greets the senses upon atomisation.

Heart Notes
Jasmine · Lavender · Saffron

The beating soul of the fragrance, developing over several hours on skin.

Base Notes
Mysore Sandalwood · Oakmoss · Musk

The deep, enduring foundation that anchors the composition and forms the trail.
