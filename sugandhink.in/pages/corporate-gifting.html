The Maison

Why Sugandh Ink for
Corporate Gifting?

In a marketplace of generic corporate gifts, Sugandh Ink offers something genuinely uncommon: the gift of olfactory architecture. Each fragrance in our library represents months of maturation, rare botanicals sourced directly from Indian growers, and a centuries-old tradition of extraction that cannot be replicated.

Our corporate gifting programme is designed for organisations that value craft, provenance, and the subtle art of making a lasting impression. Every gift is an invitation into the world of artisanal Indian perfumery.

Bespoke Curation

Every corporate gift is curated by our scent architects to align with your brand's character - whether understated elegance, bold presence, or warm approachability.

Custom Packaging

Full branding capability including engraved glass, embossed packaging, and branded outer boxes. Your corporate identity, realised in the Sugandh Ink atelier.

Volume Flexibility

From intimate gifts for board members to large-scale employee appreciation programmes. Minimum order quantity of 10 units; no upper limit.
