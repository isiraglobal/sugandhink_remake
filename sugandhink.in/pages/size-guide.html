Size Guide

Choose the perfect bottle for your olfactory journey. From pocket-friendly travel to the complete Prestige experience.

Size Name Approx. Sprays Best For
10 ml Travel ~120 sprays Exploring new scents, travel, occasional wear
30 ml Sample ~360 sprays Getting acquainted with a composition, rotation wear
50 ml Standard ~600 sprays Daily signature, long-term intimate wear
100 ml Prestige ~1,200 sprays The definitive experience, collector’s piece, gifting
10 ml
Travel

A pocket-sized companion. Fits any bag and allows you to carry the atelier with you. Ideal for sampling a composition before committing to a full bottle.

30 ml
Sample

A generous introduction. Lasts several months of regular wear and lets you experience the full evolution of a fragrance across seasons.

50 ml
Standard

Our most popular size. The sweet spot between commitment and variety. 600 sprays offer 6–8 months of daily use.

100 ml
Prestige

The ultimate homage to a composition you cannot live without. A year’s supply housed in our premium bottle with an engraved collar.

Our Recommendation

If you are discovering a new accord, begin with the 10 ml Travel size. For compositions you already know and love, the 50 ml Standard offers the finest balance of presence and value. The 100 ml Prestige is reserved for the rare perfumes that become part of your identity.
