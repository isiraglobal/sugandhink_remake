Bespoke
Enquiry

A fragrance created entirely around you - your memory, your skin, your signature. The process takes three to six months and results in a composition that belongs to no one else.

The Process

01

The Consultation

A one-hour conversation - in person at our Mumbai studio or via video. We explore your scent memories, preferred olfactory families, occasions, and emotional intention. No brief is too abstract.

02

The Accord Development

Our nose develops three preliminary accords based on the consultation. These are presented to you as 3ml samples with detailed notes on construction. One accord is chosen and refined.

03

The Refinement Rounds

Up to three rounds of modification - adjusting the top note profile, heart evolution, or base longevity. You sign off on the final formula.

04

The Delivery

Your bespoke composition is produced in a minimum of 50ml and delivered in bespoke packaging with a numbered certificate of uniqueness. The formula is registered in your name - we will never recreate it for another client.
