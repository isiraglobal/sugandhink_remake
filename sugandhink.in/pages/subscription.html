- Questions

Frequently Asked

Can I skip a month?
Yes. You can pause your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. Your subscription will resume automatically on your next billing cycle unless you choose to extend the pause.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees.
What if I don't like a fragrance?
We carefully curate based on your preferences, but taste is personal. If a fragrance does not resonate with you, we welcome your feedback so our perfumers can refine future selections.
Can I swap a bottle for something else?
Our curation is designed to expand your olfactory horizons. However, if you have a strong preference against a specific note, please let us know in your preferences and we will accommodate.
