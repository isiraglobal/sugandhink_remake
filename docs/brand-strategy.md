# Aurelia Brand Strategy 2026

## 1. Core Vision
To redefine luxury through "The Extraordinary Essence" – a philosophy that every scent should be an immersive journey into a different dimension of reality.

## 2. Target Audience
- **The Modern Aesthete**: Individuals who value sensory depth and digital immersion.
- **The Luxury Collector**: Those who seek limited-run, artisan-crafted experiences.

## 3. Brand Pillars
- **Cinematic Purity**: Every digital touchpoint must feel like a frame from a high-end film.
- **Botanical Isolation**: Sourcing rare molecules that exist only in specific micro-climates.
- **Spatial Storytelling**: Moving away from flat e-commerce towards 3D-staged scrollytelling.

## 4. Visual Language
- **Colors**: Obsidian Black, Aurelia Gold, Mist White.
- **Transitions**: Fan masks, depth-zooms, and atmospheric fades.
